NBA: The Ultimate Basketball Competition

The National Basketball Association (NBA) is the premier men's professional basketball league in North America. Since its establishment in 1946, the NBA has expanded from eight to thirty teams. NBA games are widely followed in North America, and internationally, with over one billion viewers in over 200 countries in 2015.

The NBA Finals, consisting of a best-of-seven playoff series, determines the NBA champion each year.

Michael Jordan: A Basketball Icon

Michael Jeffrey Jordan (born February 17, 1963) is an American professional basketball coach and retired player who is widely regarded as one of the greatest basketball players of all time. He played his entire career with the Chicago Bulls, and is considered one of the top six players in NBA history by Sports Illustrated.
